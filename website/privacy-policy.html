Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

At NextGen Solutions, protecting your privacy is embedded in our edge architecture. We rigorously enforce zero-trust policies, collect minimal information, and ensure secure, encrypted transit (TLS 1.3) through Cloudflare.

1. Data Collection

We do not use aggressive third-party trackers. Standard analytics are processed directly at the edge to respect user privacy and adhere to global data compliance laws (GDPR, CCPA).

2. Security Standards

Our servers employ strict CSPs (Content Security Policies), mandate HTTPS upgrades, enforce HSTS, and utilize .well-known/security.txt protocols for ethical security reporting. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.

3. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our data protection team via our Contact page.
